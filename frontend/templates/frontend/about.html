Dave Wells

I am a multi-talented human being. I describe myself as a jack of all trades and am curious by nature, which lends itself well to a field with constantly evolving technology and a need for a variety of skillset. I have a very unique cross-discipline education spanning the Social Sciences, Computer Science, and Art. In 2013 I graduated from Fort Lewis College with a Bachelors in Sociology. This led me to return to school to study Computer Science at CSU.

My desire to be a lifelong learner which led me down my career path is also very prevalent throughout the rest of my life. I explore woodworking, painting, pottery & many other skill based hobbies in my free time.
